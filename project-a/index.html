Greed

Scientific Name:

Infinitus Avaritia

Discovered by / at:

Discovered by Michael Liu at a ruined castle

Family:

Has no family.

Habitat:

Have only been discovered where there are purple skies

Appearance:

A frog-like creature that can grow rapidly and turn yellow when fed gold

Key Characteristics:

  • Greedy for money
  • Will grow when fed
  • Jump height decreases as it grows bigger
  • After a certain size the frog will revert to original state

What we know so far about Greed. Greed is a creature that is obsessive over money, its entire personality and size revolves around it. More testing will be needed to determine other traits
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Only form of interaction right now with the creature is to feed it golden coins, other non hostile actions to the creature have no reaction.
